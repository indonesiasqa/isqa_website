Indonesian Software Quality Assurance

We are Software Quality Assurance organization based in Jakarta, Indonesia.

We mostly do meetups, but conferences and workshops are underway to be one of our major activities.

Community

We aspire to be one of Indonesia's biggest testing communities through our meetups and workshops.

Meet-ups

Our meetups are one of our ways to transfer valuable knowledge from various talents across Indonesia and abroad.

Connections

Other than knowlege transfers, we'd love to be the organization that connects people for next big opportunities.
